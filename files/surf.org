* Materials:
**** Corn Starch
**** Acrylic Stucco
**** Sticker sheet
**** tree sap
**** large tray (for salt water soak)
**** microcrystalline wax
**** coconut oil
**** petroleum jelly
**** beeswax
* Process:
1. Mix the following
   a. 1 part acrylic stucco
   b. 1/4 part water
   c. 1/8 part corn starch
   d. 1/8 part tree sap
2. Spray the above materials on the sheet and let dry
3. soak the sheet in salt water for 12 hours
4. Mix the oil and wax soluton
   a. 1/2 part microcrystalline wax
   b. 1/8 part coconut oil
   c. 1/8 part petroleum jelly
   d. 1/4 part beeswax
5. Apply oil and wax solution
